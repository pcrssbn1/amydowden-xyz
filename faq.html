Frequently Asked Questions About Amy Dowden

Amy Dowden has captured the hearts of millions through her exceptional dancing on Strictly Come Dancing and her courageous openness about her health challenges. As a Welsh professional dancer who has competed at the highest levels of ballroom dancing, Amy brings both technical excellence and genuine warmth to everything she does. Her journey has been marked by remarkable achievements on the dance floor alongside significant personal battles with chronic illness.

Since joining Strictly Come Dancing in 2017, Amy has become one of the show's most beloved professionals, known for her creative choreography and supportive teaching style. However, her impact extends far beyond entertainment. Living with Crohn's disease since childhood and facing a breast cancer diagnosis in 2023, Amy has used her platform to raise awareness about health issues affecting millions. Her transparency about these struggles has made her an inspiration to people facing similar challenges.

Who is Amy Dowden?

Amy Dowden is a Welsh professional ballroom and Latin dancer best known for being a professional dancer on the BBC show Strictly Come Dancing since 2017. Born on August 10, 1990, in Caerphilly, Wales, she began dancing at age eight and went on to become a four-time British National Champion in ballroom dancing. She made history as the first Welsh professional dancer on Strictly Come Dancing and has partnered with celebrities including Karim Zeroual, with whom she reached the semi-finals in 2019. Beyond her television work, Amy is a prominent health advocate, openly discussing her experiences with Crohn's disease and breast cancer to raise awareness and support others facing similar challenges.

What health condition does Amy Dowden have?

Amy Dowden has Crohn's disease, an inflammatory bowel condition that she has been open about managing throughout her dancing career. She was diagnosed at age 11 and has dealt with the condition's challenges for over two decades. Crohn's disease causes inflammation in the digestive tract, leading to symptoms including severe abdominal pain, fatigue, and nutritional deficiencies. Amy has been hospitalized multiple times due to flare-ups and has spoken candidly about performing on live television while experiencing symptoms. In 2023, she faced an additional health challenge when diagnosed with breast cancer at age 32, which required chemotherapy treatment. She announced she was cancer-free in 2024 and has since focused on recovery and gradually returning to dancing.

Is Amy Dowden married?

Yes, Amy Dowden is married to Ben Jones, and they tied the knot in July 2022 after being together for several years. Ben is also a professional dancer, and the couple met through the competitive ballroom dancing circuit. Their wedding took place on July 23, 2022, in Carmarthenshire, Wales, with many of Amy's Strictly Come Dancing colleagues in attendance. Ben has been a crucial support system for Amy throughout her health challenges, particularly during her cancer diagnosis and treatment in 2023. He has accompanied her to medical appointments and adjusted his own professional schedule to be present during her treatment. Their shared understanding of the demands of professional dancing has created a strong foundation for their relationship.

How old is Amy Dowden and when did she start dancing?

Amy Dowden was born on August 10, 1990, making her 34 years old as of 2024. She began her dancing journey at the age of eight in her hometown of Caerphilly, Wales. From those early beginnings, she quickly showed exceptional talent and dedication to ballroom and Latin dancing. By her teenage years, she was competing at national and international levels, eventually winning the British National Championships four times. Her early start in dance, combined with decades of training and competition experience, provided the foundation for her successful career as a professional dancer. Despite being diagnosed with Crohn's disease at age 11, just three years after starting dance, Amy never let the condition derail her passion and continued to pursue dancing at the highest competitive levels.

What is Amy Dowden's latest cancer update?

Amy Dowden announced in February 2024 that she had completed her cancer treatment and received the news that she had no evidence of disease, effectively declaring her cancer-free. She was diagnosed with breast cancer in April 2023 after discovering a lump just before her honeymoon. The diagnosis led to her withdrawal from Strictly Come Dancing Series 21 in 2023 while she underwent chemotherapy and other treatments. Throughout her treatment journey, Amy documented her experience on social media, sharing both the difficult moments and the support she received. According to information from Breast Cancer Now, early detection significantly improves outcomes, and Amy has since become an advocate for regular self-examination and cancer screening. She has expressed her intention to return to dancing and Strictly Come Dancing when medically cleared, focusing on rebuilding her strength and stamina after the intensive treatment regimen.

Who has Amy Dowden been partnered with on Strictly Come Dancing?

Amy Dowden has been partnered with several celebrities during her time on Strictly Come Dancing since joining in 2017. Her first partner was actor and comedian Brian Conley in Series 15, followed by actor Danny John-Jules (best known for Red Dwarf) in Series 16. Her most successful partnership came in Series 17 (2019) with CBBC presenter Karim Zeroual, reaching the semi-finals and finishing in fourth place with consistently high scores. In Series 18 (2020), she partnered with Royal Marines veteran and TV presenter JJ Chalmers, reaching the quarter-finals. Series 19 (2021) saw her paired with McFly musician Tom Fletcher, though they had to withdraw in Week 5 due to positive COVID-19 tests. She was unable to compete in Series 21 (2023) due to her cancer treatment but participated in group dances when her health permitted.

What is Amy Dowden's connection to Wales?

Amy Dowden was born and raised in Caerphilly, Wales, and maintains a strong connection to her Welsh heritage. She made history as the first Welsh professional dancer on Strictly Come Dancing when she joined the show in 2017, a distinction she has said makes her particularly proud. Throughout her career, she has been an ambassador for Welsh culture and has spoken about the importance of representing Wales on such a prominent platform. Amy started her dancing career in Wales, training at local dance schools before competing nationally and internationally. She married her husband Ben Jones in Wales, choosing Carmarthenshire as the location for their 2022 wedding. Amy frequently references her Welsh roots in interviews and on social media, and she has become a role model for young dancers in Wales who aspire to reach professional levels in ballroom and Latin dancing.
